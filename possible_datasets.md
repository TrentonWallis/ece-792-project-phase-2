**Log of possible image datasets**

- Standard CIFAR-10: https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~kriz/cifar.html (Very very simple dataset)
- - The CIFAR-10 dataset consists of 60000 32x32 colour images in 10 classes, with 6000 images per class. There are 50000 training images and 10000 test images.
- Flickr Image dataset from kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hsankesara/flickr-image-dataset
- - This dataset has ~30k images of varying sizes. There are also scene description annotations, but we won't need those.
- - Flickr dataset is far more diverse than CIFAR-10.
